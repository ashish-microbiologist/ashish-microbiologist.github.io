About Our Research Lab

Advanced Microbiology Research Facility

Established in 2010, our Microbiology Research Lab has been at the forefront of microbial sciences research. We specialize in pathogen identification, antibiotic resistance studies, and environmental microbiology.

Our lab is equipped with state-of-the-art instrumentation and follows strict biosafety protocols (BSL-2 certified). We collaborate with universities, hospitals, and research institutions worldwide.
